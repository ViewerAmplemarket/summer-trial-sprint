SUMMER TRIAL SPRINT

A partner SPIFF that pays per trial started, Jun 29 - Sep 30, 2026.

This is a sprint, not a marathon: bring us leads that are ready to move, and earn an escalating bonus the moment a referred account starts a real trial - on top of your standard commission.

How the sprint works

Five things to know before you start sending leads. The faster and more selectively you work, the more you make.

✓

Paid at trial start

You earn when a referred account starts a real trial - not at lead, not at demo. The lead and demo get you there; the bonus lands when the account actually starts trialing.

✓

Stacks on top

The SPIFF is extra. It stacks on top of your standard commission - think of it as a Summer booster, not a replacement.

✓

The rate climbs

Your bonus is cumulative across the campaign. Each tier you hit pushes the per-trial rate higher, so momentum pays. A handful of hot, fast-moving referrals will out-earn a long list of cold ones.

✓

The 30-day clock

Every lead you submit starts a 30-day countdown. If the account hasn't started a trial by day 30, it's out - no bonus, no extensions. Thirty days is the ceiling, not a target. Send leads you believe will trial soon, not leads you hope might trial someday.

✓

Real trials only

A qualifying trial needs genuine product usage. If a trial starts but we record zero usage - no logins, no activity - it does not count. We verify usage before paying.

The payout ladder

Cumulative across the campaign - your rate steps up as you hit each tier. There is no cap on how much you can earn.

✓

Trials 1-2 → $250 per trial

Your first and second qualified trials each earn $250.

✓

Trials 3-4 → $350 per trial

Trials three and four each earn $350.

✓

Trials 5-6 → $450 per trial

Trials five and six each earn $450.

✓

Trials 7+ → $550 per trial

Every trial from the seventh onward earns $550. No cap - keep driving fast-moving trials and keep climbing.

Eligibility & what counts

Open to all active partners. Here is exactly what makes a trial count toward your payout.

✓

Who's eligible

All active partners. Submit leads through your normal referral flow.

✓

The submission window

The lead must be submitted between Jun 29 and Sep 30, 2026. After Sep 30, no new leads count - but any lead submitted in-window still has its full 30-day clock to convert.

✓

The 30-day rule

The account must start a trial within 30 days of your submission. Day 31 with no trial means no bonus.

✓

Real usage required

The trial must show actual product usage. A trial with zero usage - no logins, no activity - does not qualify, even if it technically started.

✓

Net-new, ICP-fit only

The account must be a real, ICP-fit prospect that is not already an Amplemarket customer nor a previously submitted opportunity. Existing customers and previous submissions do not qualify.

✓

One payout per account

One bonus per unique account, deduped. Referring the same company twice doesn't double the payout. Trial numbering is cumulative per partner and resets at campaign start, ordered by trial date.

FAQ

The questions partners ask most. Still stuck? Reach the partnerships team at partners@amplemarket.com.

1

When does the sprint run?

Submissions open Jun 29 and close Sep 30, 2026. After that, no new leads count - but any lead submitted in-window still has its full 30-day clock to convert.

2

What's this 30-day thing exactly?

From the moment you submit a lead, the account has 30 days to start a trial. Day 31 with no trial means no bonus. It's the single most important rule of the sprint, so pick leads that are ready to move and serious about getting started with Amplemarket.

3

A lead I sent in late September trials in October - does it still count?

Yes, as long as the trial starts within 30 days of your submission. A lead submitted Sep 30 has until roughly the end of October. We reconcile final payouts once those clocks close.

4

What counts as a "real" trial?

A trial with genuine product usage. A trial that starts but records zero usage - no logins, no activity - doesn't qualify. We check usage before paying out.

5

When exactly do I earn - lead, demo, or trial?

At trial start. The lead and demo get you there; the bonus lands when the account starts a real trial.

6

Does this replace my normal commission?

No. It's on top of your standard commission. Think of it as a Summer booster.

7

How is my "trial number" counted?

Cumulatively across the whole campaign. Your first two qualifying trials pay $250 each, trials three and four pay $350, and so on up the ladder.

8

What if I refer an account that's already a customer?

Existing customers don't qualify. Bonuses are for net-new, ICP-fit accounts only, deduped to one payout per account.

9

How and when am I paid?

We'll message you every Friday with your cumulative trial count, current ladder step, and projected payout. Weekly figures are preliminary and reconciled at payout. Bonuses are issued monthly via PartnerStack, alongside your usual commission.

10

Is there a cap?

No cap. Keep driving fast-moving trials and keep climbing the ladder.
